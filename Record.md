One of the aggregate data structures (or compound data type), which means it's basically a value that contains other values.
Also called a **tuple** or **struct**, depending on the language and context.
# Accessing the data
The elements of a record (called **fields** or **members**) are usually named, which means you can access each of them by providing their name.
