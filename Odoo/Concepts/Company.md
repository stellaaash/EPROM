In Odoo, a company is **an individual business entity that operates independently**.
It has its own legal identity, financial records, and specific operational settings.
# Branches
Branches represent **subdivisions within a company**, like regional offices or departments which **all operate under the same company**.
# Multi-Company
Odoo allows its users to configure multiple companies under the same database.
This permits sharing of records **across companies** and aggregate data between them.
